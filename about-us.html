About Us

We’re building the best network of trusted pet sitters and dog walkers

If you’re a pet parent, you may need services such as pet boarding, pet sitting, or dog walking. Because we’re pet people, the idea of Sidelick stemmed from a need we had ourselves. The first step towards our launch was to recognize that the daily needs of our pets may exceed what we can consistently offer as individuals over time.

Some studies indicate “pet ownership in its current form is likely unsustainable in a growing, urbanized population” (Source: Rault, 2015). Our urban environments are evolving in a way that shapes faster lifestyles, inducing smaller living spaces and less time for pet parents to care for their pets, hence affecting their overall quality of life.

We know you love your pets, and we understand that there are times where you‘ll be too busy to cater for their needs or you’ll need to be away for a short while. Sidelick is here to help through an online platform that connects you to a network of pet lovers around you, always ready to assist.

All our pet sitters and dog walkers are rigorously vetted and trained. They provide the best care available to your pets and will always have access to 24/7 vet assistance and insurance coverage during their mission. So whenever you need a paw, just look around Sidelick! Relax, we’ve got you covered.
